Frequently Asked Questions

How do I post an item?

Go to “My Listings” and click “Post Item” to create a new listing.

Do I need an EMU email?

Yes. Only EMU students with verified EMU email addresses can use the platform.

Is there a fee for selling items?

No. EMU Marketplace is completely free for students.

Can I buy official EMU merchandise here?

Yes! Visit the EMU Store section for sweaters, hoodies, water bottles, and more.
