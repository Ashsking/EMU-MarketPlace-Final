About EMU Marketplace

EMU Marketplace is the official student-to-student buying and selling platform created for Eastern Mennonite University. Students can buy EMU merchandise, post their own items, make offers, and connect securely through their EMU accounts.

Our goal is to create a safe, efficient, and student-focused marketplace that supports campus life and sustainability by giving items a second home.
