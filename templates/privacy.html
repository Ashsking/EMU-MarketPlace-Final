Privacy Policy

The EMU Student Marketplace collects only the information necessary to create and manage your account, including your name, EMU email address, and login credentials. Your information is used solely to support marketplace features such as posting listings, completing purchases, and accessing your dashboard.

We do not share your personal information with third parties outside of EMU. However, your posted listings, including item descriptions and images, are visible to all marketplace users. Please avoid sharing sensitive or private details in your listings.

Cookies and session data are used to maintain your login status and improve your browsing experience. By continuing to use the marketplace, you consent to this data being used as described.

If you have concerns about your privacy or wish to request account removal, please contact EMU IT or the marketplace administrator.
